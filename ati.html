ATI

ATI ATI Technologies is a Canadian company, developer and supplier of graphics processors and motherboard chipsets, operating from 1985 to 2006 as an independent company, one of the largest in its industry. In 2006, ATI was acquired by AMD Corporation and became a graphic division of AMD Graphics Products Group; ATI products continued to be produced under the previous brand. Since the end of 2010, all products are manufactured under the AMD brand.
